# Playground

## Purpose
Provide a playful space for open-ended exploration that is less constrained than the lab.

## What belongs here
- Wild ideas
- Visual jokes without immediate canon intent
- Early stylistic mashups
- Alternative humor approaches
- Concept sketches for future collections
- Rapid-fire experiments

## Rules
- Playground is exploratory, not canonical.
- Ideas here may be rough, contradictory, or intentionally strange.
- Successful playground experiments can move to the lab.
- Only lab-validated ideas may move to validation or canon.

## Outcome states
- Keep playing
- Refine in lab
- Reject
- Promote to lab
