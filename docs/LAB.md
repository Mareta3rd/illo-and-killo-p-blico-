# Lab

## Purpose
Provide a safe space for experimentation before ideas enter canon.

## What belongs here
- Alternative prompts
- New composition ideas
- Style tests
- Color experiments
- Unusual parodies
- Technical trials
- 3D prototypes
- Automation experiments

## Rules
- Lab work is not canon by default.
- Anything in the lab must be clearly marked as experimental.
- Experiments can fail without cost to the canon.
- Successful experiments may later move to validation or canon.

## Outcome states
- Rejected
- Needs revision
- Prototype
- Ready for validation
- Promote to canon
